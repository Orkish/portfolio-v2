Building awesomeness in Silicon Alley

Ever since I was a kid, I have been curiously taking things apart and attempting to put them back together. Most of the time they would never be what they once were or just left as a pile of rubble. This curiosity carried with me as I grew up. As I went from taking apart action figures and alarm clocks to PC's and car engines, I am more meticulous about putting things back together now. This drive to learn more inevitably lead me to programming, where breaking things was okay and even encouraged at times.

I am a front-end developer with an insatiable hunger to learn about web development. Naturally while working on the front-end, I became very intrigued with back-end functionality and data storage. I have built a few web apps with various full-stack and worked with different databases to enchance the front-end experience. While immersed in the development culture, I find that there is never a dull moment and you are always learning. This is the reason why I love what I do, this is is why I became a developer.
