David's Bridal

Worked on David's Bridal #getglam sweepstake campaign for their prom promotion special while working for Offerpop. PSD mock up was delivered by client with specification and requirements. Once users have signed up, they would automatically be entered for a chance to win. This campaign has social shareability, data capture and discount codes. I was tasked to turn the PSD into a working application which lived on the client's Facebook page. Campaigns life cycle will only last for the duration of the sweepstake which in this case a few months.

  • PSD Mock Up
  • UX/UI Design
  • CSS3
  • jQuery
  • Data Capture
  • Social Share API
  • Custom Form Validation
