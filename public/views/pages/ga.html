Front-End Web Development

General Assembly (GA) is an unconventional, bootcamp style, learning insitution. Their goal is to turn people into designers, developers, project manangers and so much more in a fraction of the time it would traditionally take. I work at GA as an Expert in Residence/co-instructor for the Front-End Web Development (FEWD) program. FEWD is a 10 weeks long, part-time program which aims to teach students web development fundamentals. Learn more about FEWD and check out our resources site for the students.
